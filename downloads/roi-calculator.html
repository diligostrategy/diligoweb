Calculation Methodology

These projections are based on aggregated industry benchmarks from multilingual content programs. Actual results will vary based on content quality, market competition, and execution.

  • Traffic multiplier: Each additional language typically generates 40-70% of the original language's organic traffic within 12 months (we use 55% as a mid-range estimate).
  • Localization cost factor: Localization into each additional language typically costs 35-50% of the original content creation cost (we use 40%).
  • Conversion rate adjustment: Localized content in the user's native language increases conversion rates by approximately 70% compared to non-localized content (CSA Research).
  • Ramp-up factor: International SEO results are averaged over 12 months, accounting for the 3-6 month ramp-up period for new language versions to gain traction.
  • Content velocity: We assume your budget allows for consistent monthly content production across all target languages.
